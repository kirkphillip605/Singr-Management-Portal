The app is divided across four subdomains: singrkaraoke.com (landing), host.singrkaraoke.com (singer portal), admin.singrkaraoke.com (admin/support), and api.singrkaraoke.com (API layer).

Phase 2: Unified Better Auth & Database Schema Setup

Phase 3: Subdomain Routing, Middleware, & Onboarding Workflows
